Features

Cloud 6 is a perfect music site for independent musicians

Music Uploader

You can upload your self-created music to our site. All you need to do is choose your music from your local directory then filling up music information form. After that you are good to go!

Music Player

You do want others to listen to your masterpiece. With our site, it is just one simple click on the title of a song.

And More...

User can rate songs they've listened, find more music on genre page or find most rated songs at home page...

Team 6 -- Clound 6

the rest of the world. We offer a free platform for downloading and uploading songs,
and are dedicated tohelping up-and-coming artists be discovered!
