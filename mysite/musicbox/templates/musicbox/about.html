About us

Cloud 6 is the premier destination for indie music artists to connect with the rest of the world. We offer
a free platform for downloading and uploading songs, and are dedicated to helping up-and-coming artists
be discovered!

Cloud 6 was established in September 2015 by a team of six hard-working individuals: Fan Liu, Collin Qiao,
Janice Sargent, Zhi Chen, Wen Du, and Yuchen Tang. We take great pride in our website, and are even
prouder of people who use it.
